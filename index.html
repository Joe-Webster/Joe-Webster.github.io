Joe Webster


About:

I am an assistant professor in Mathematics at Grinnell College. I am interested in number theory, combinatorics, mathematical physics, and the interplay between them.

Before coming to Grinnell, I was a postdoc in Mathematics at University of Virginia. I received my PhD in Mathematics at University of Oregon in June of 2021. Here is my CV (last updated August 2023).
